The Homepage

Users who have created their account will be greeted to this page when logging in. This page is your meal planner. Here you will be able to view all your upcoming meals and be able to prepare them ahead of time! Every meal is categorized into their specific days and represented by a window. You can easily view and your downloaded recipes by clicking on each window.

Navigation

The bottom of each page shows the navigation bar. Designed to be as simple as possible, it contains the most important functions of the app. These include (from left to right) The Homepage, Recipe Finder, Create Recipe, Carbon footprint Analysis and Profile Page.

Recipe Finder

This page is simply a place to find inspiration for your next meal. Here you will be able to browse recipes from all over the world. Click on the recipe of your choice and browse its contents. We have also placed a filter button for your convenience if you want to browse specific ingredients and/or diets. This app learns about your preferences over time and will show meals most relevant for you at the top of the page.

Analysis:

The gem of our app is here at the analysis page. Here we stockpile info about the ingredients from your every recorded meal and translate them into our calculator. On the page you will find info about the resources you have saved, and they will be updated from every meal.

Profile

The profile page shows your name and description about you and your diet style. The preferences button allows you to toggle between what diet plan you want to pursue. This will help the algorithm in the recipe finder browse relevant meals for you! You are free to change your diet settings at any time.
If you have any questions about what info we collect and use, visis our FAQ-page. The button that takes you to the End User License Agreement is available at the bottom of the page in "Privacy" window.
