FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What information does EcoFeast collect from me?

EcoFeast only collects information you consent to giving us. For example when you register on EcoFeast, we collect your email-address. We also provide you with the option to share your experiences with EcoFeast (such as posting meals) on any of your social media accounts, such as Instagram and Facebook. This is also information we collect from you, although we only use it for this purpose.


What does EcoFeast use my information for?

EcoFeast uses your information whenever we require feedback from you regarding your experiences with our app, or for when we want to contact you. We also use your information in order to enable user-to-user communications, however with consent from both parties, this may include your name.


Does EcoFeast share my information with other companies?

No, we will never share your information with other companies for monetary gain. We will only share information whenever we are legally obligated to do so.


I don't want to use EcoFeast anymore, what happens to my data?

When you terminate your account with us, the information we keep with us will also be removed. So rest assured, your information is yours to keep!
