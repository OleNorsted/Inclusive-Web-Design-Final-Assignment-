The Challenge

Veganthing faces a variety of problems and concerns in regards to climate change:

  1. – Unsustainable agriculture, forestry, and water use.
  2. – Being climate-friendly requires a lifestyle change.
  3. – Lack of information and guidance.
  4. – Contribution is not visible.

Tackling the climate problem is not easy.

The problem becomes more apparent year after year, as we see more extreme weather, melting of the poles and deserts becoming larger. These alarming consequences of climate change can traced back to human behavior, and a lack of unsustainable habits as simple as eating [2].

All of us are encouraged to reduce our ecological footprint. We pressure politicians into making changes for the better, donate to various climate change causes and most countries have become incredibly good at recycling. What most if not all of us have in common though, is that we like to eat. Most people do not know that methane has a global warming potential, 86 times greater than that of CO2 on a year time frame. And what makes up for much of the planet's methane production? Agriculture [3].

The Social Problem

The climate problem is not new.

In today’s age we’re surrounded by articles discussing the possible end of civilization as we know it due to climate changes. While the average person might say that they care about climate changes, their actions may speak differently. It’s difficult for people to change their lifestyle choices in hopes of stopping climate change due to the lack of time and resources. The lack of diverse meals that cut out foods that help increase the speed of climate changes makes it difficult for people to make full healthy meals that taste good while saving the planet. We believe in creating recipes that dabble in different types of diets is an easy way for people to cut down on their emissions while providing healthy meals.


The apps main goal is information.

We see that info about non-meat options are scarce and difficult to find. Thats why our ambition is to give people the option to share their info, catalogue their progress. This will, inspire others to be creative towards the subtle art of food.

The Solution

Veganthing will tackle these climate change problems by:

  • - Making sustainable agriculture, forestry and water-use possible for the average joe.
  • - Providing a smooth transition towards a more eco-friendly and healthier lifestyle.
  • - Providing information and guidance in the form of sharing recipes and articles that relates to good, non-meat food options.
  • - Tracing and making the resources you have saved over time visible.

Eco-friendliness made easy!

“appnavn” introduces new incredible recipes, and connects you to other like-minded individuals near and far. Prepare meals far in advance with our meal-planner. Expand your cooking skills with our search and share function, where hand-picked and random recipes from across the world is in view. Our ambition is to get involved in climate change in a fun and healthy way. We tackle the climate challenge by making it easy for everyone to change to a non-meat lifestyle, with custom settings for each user that can be changed at any time. By implementing our calculator, we can make your carbon footprint trackable. We do this by tracking every meal you add with our app to your over-all contribution towards the climate [4] [5]. The meal-plan, from pescatarian to vegan, is up to you to decide. Together we can turn the tide and fight climate change, one meal at a time.
