Inspired by the UN’s 13th Sustainable development goal – Climate Action.

The Challenge

EcoFeast faces a variety of problems and concerns regarding climate change:

  1. – Unsustainable agriculture, forestry, and water use.
  2. – Being climate-friendly requires a lifestyle change.
  3. – Lack of information and guidance.
  4. – Contribution is not visible.

Tackling the climate problem is not easy.

The problem becomes more apparent year after year, as we see more extreme weather, melting of the poles and deserts becoming larger. These alarming consequences of climate change can traced back to human behavior, and a lack of unsustainable habits as simple as eating [2].

All of us are encouraged to reduce our ecological footprint. We pressure politicians into making changes in the community, donate to various climate change causes and recycling has become a bigger part of society than ever before. What most if not all of us have in common though, is that we like to eat. As global food production rises at the same rate as the population, so too does methane gas production. This gas has a global warming potential of 86 times greater than that of carbon dioxide on a yearly time frame. And what makes up for much of the planet's methane production? Agriculture [3].

The Social Problem

The climate problem is not new.

In today’s age we’re surrounded by articles discussing the possible end of civilization as we know it due to climate changes. While the average person might say that they care about climate changes, their actions may speak differently. It’s difficult for people to change their lifestyle choices in hopes of stopping climate change due to the lack of time and resources. The lack of diverse meals that cut out foods that help increase the speed of climate changes makes it difficult for people to make full healthy meals that taste good while saving the planet [4]. We believe in creating recipes that dabble in different types of diets is an easy way for people to cut down on their emissions while providing healthy meals.


Our main goals are to bring information and inspiring small changes.

We see that info about non-meat options are scarce and difficult to find. Thats why our ambition is to give people the option to share their info, catalogue their progress. This will inspire others to be creative and open towards the subtle art of food.

The Solution

EcoFeast will tackle these climate change problems by:

  • - Making sustainable use of resources into an easy and fun task.
  • - Providing a smooth transition towards a more eco-friendly and healthier lifestyle.
  • - Providing great information and guidance towards eco-friendliness.
  • - Making your contributions visible.

Eco-friendliness made easy!

EcoFeast’s job is to introduce you to new fun and interesting recipes while connecting you to other like-minded people, whether they’re in your vicinity or not. Prepare your meals in advance with our meal-planner or expand your horizons in the cooking world with our search and share function with recipes from all over the world!

Our amibition is to help people get involved in climate change in a way that’s fun and healthy. We want to do this by introducing a varied diet that doesn’t just consist of meat, and take away the stigma surrounding a non meat lifestyle [5]. With the help of our calculator, you can track your own carbon footprint by adding your meals into our app and see live how each of your contributions help! All of us can turn the tide of climate change, one meal at a time! Visit the EcoFeast app
