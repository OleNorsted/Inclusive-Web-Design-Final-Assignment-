How to get started

Step 1

Start by downloading our app, and begin making your personalised account. Set up your preferred diet and choose between vegan, vegetarian and pescatarian.

Step 2

Customize your mealplan and add them to your schedule! Plan ahead weeks or months at a time. Choose between recipes on the internet, or recipes others have created!

Step 3

Decide your mealplan and view the recipes you have chosen!
